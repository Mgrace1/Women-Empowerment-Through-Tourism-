About Us

Empowering women through sustainable tourism and creating opportunities for leadership and growth.

Who We Are

WETS connects women to opportunities in the tourism sector, offering training, mentorship, and resources to help them thrive.

We foster sustainable tourism practices, emphasizing environmental preservation and cultural respect.

Our community supports collaboration and innovation, bringing together women from diverse backgrounds.

WETS is dedicated to transforming tourism into a tool for empowerment and positive change.

Our Mission

To empower women through education, skill-building, and access to opportunities in the tourism industry, fostering independence and economic growth.

Our Vision

A world where women play a leading role in sustainable tourism, transforming communities and creating a lasting impact.
